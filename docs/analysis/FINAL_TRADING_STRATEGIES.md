## üéì LESSONS LEARNED

### 1. Parser Bug Impact
- **Issue:** 21.2% of signals (191/900) were misclassified as LONG when they were SHORT
- **Impact:** SHORT signals showed 0.5% WR (incorrect) before fix
- **Lesson:** Always validate entry vs target price to determine position type
- **Fix:** Changed parser to compare entry_price > target1 = SHORT

### 2. Thursday Curse is Real
- **LONG:** 33.3% WR on Thursday (worst day)
- **SHORT:** 22.2% WR on Thursday (worst day)
- **Combined:** ~30% WR on Thursday
- **Action:** AVOID all Thursday signals, no exceptions

### 3. Time of Day Matters
- **LONG:** 02:00 UTC is 3x better than 00:00 UTC (76.8% vs 25.0%)
- **SHORT:** 06:00 UTC is 3x better than 16:00 UTC (69.6% vs 22.6%)
- **Lesson:** Timing is as important as the coin itself

### 4. Not All Coins Are Equal
- **BNB LONG:** 88.9% WR vs **ADA LONG:** 39.4% WR
- **FET SHORT:** 100% WR vs **XTZ SHORT:** 0% WR
- **Lesson:** Coin selection can double your win rate

### 5. Filtering Works
- **LONG:** 50.6% baseline ‚Üí 82.1% filtered (+31.5%)
- **SHORT:** 46.8% baseline ‚Üí 84.6% filtered (+37.8%)
- **Lesson:** Quality over quantity - fewer trades, higher accuracy

### 6. Sample Size Matters
- Perfect 100% coins often have only 3 signals
- Use tiered approach: prioritize coins with 5+ signals and >65% WR
- Be cautious with <3 signal sample sizes
